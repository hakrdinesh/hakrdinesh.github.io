Lunch Bug now available on Google Play

by Matt Hackett, 2012 Oct 11

While Android users have been able to play Lunch Bug in their mobile browsers since its launch, the game runs pretty slowly on some devices, and the sound is severely handicapped in the mobile browser. Now, thanks to CocoonJS, Lunch Bug runs beautifully, sounds great, and can be played without an Internet connection on Android devices.

Download Lunch Bug for Android
