Onslaught! Arena is free on Valentine's Day

by Matt Hackett, 2011 Feb 13

Love it or hate it, Valentine's Day is a day about expressing love ... and giving gifts! To celebrate and to show our love for the indie gamers out there, we're making Onslaught! Arena FREE on the Chrome Webstore for today only.

Normally $1.99, this is a great chance to save some cash for your sweetheart (or hey, for yourself!) while still getting to have some retro fun. So please help spread the word on Twitter and Facebook and happy monster slaying!
