Challenge a friend to a game of Crypt Run

by Matt Hackett, 2013 Jul 28

You can now challenge a friend to see who’s better at Crypt Run! Here’s how to do it:

  1. Visit cryptrun.lostdecadegames.com in a modern browser.
  2. Play through the cemetery dungeon.
  3. At the Game Over screen, copy the text in the “Challenge URL” input box.
  4. Send that URL to a friend via email, Twitter, or carrier pigeon.

Your friend will play the exact same procedurally-generated dungeon you played, and your two scores are compared at the end. Instant bragging rights!
