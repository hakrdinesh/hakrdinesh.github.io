Onslaught! Arena v1.2.8

by Matt Hackett, 2011 Feb 12

We just pushed a minor update to Onslaught! Arena on the Chrome Webstore. This quick update includes:

  • Removing the broken High Score table.
  • Supporting the "k" key to switch WASD and arrow keys for controls (for lefties!).
  • Supporting the "html5" keycode to unlock a (visual only) HTML5 shield. Help spread HTML5!
  • Various bug fixes and tweaks.

While this is just a small release, we've got new content in the works, including new weapons, new monsters, and a special promotion for Valentine's Day (this upcoming Monday). So stay tuned for more updates!
