Lostcast 1: Hello World

by Matt Hackett, 2011 Nov 18

Lostcast is the name of our new podcast! In this first episode we introduce ourselves, chat a little bit about Lost Decade Games, talk about the history of Onslaught!, and discuss HTML5 games in general. We’re not sure yet if it will be weekly, or monthly, or what. We’ll figure that stuff out eventually.
