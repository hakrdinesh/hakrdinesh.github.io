Lava Blade alpha 0.5.44

by Matt Hackett, 2013 Mar 25

We just sent out the latest Lava Blade alpha to our mailing list. If you signed up and haven’t received an email yet, don’t worry! We’re sending the game out in waves; we have your contact information and will get in touch shortly.

Want to sign up to alpha test?

If you haven’t signed up to be an alpha tester, you can do so by filling out this form.

Whoa, this is really different!

Yeah, if you played the previous Lava Blade alpha you may notice that things are quite different. What was once a side-scrolling platformer is now a turn-based strategy RPG. That’s quite a drastic change! Those interested in the reasoning behind this could have a listen to our recent podcast where we discussed this.

Game on!
